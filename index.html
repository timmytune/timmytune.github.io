07Why should you hire me?

Software development has been and will always be my passion. I still remember trying my hands on Visual Basic when i got my first computer! I was practically glued to the huge box and my mum's new way of punishing me was to ban me from touching the PC. This passion led me to MIT open courseware where i took my first online course (SICP Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs) and ever since then, i have taken several online courses from sites like Cousera and Udemy, studied new languages, algorithms and frameworks and read through several documentations making sure i work on projects with what i have learnt. Right now i am proficient in Javascript, Typescript and Golang. Professionally, i have been working as a software engineer since 2013 mostly working on web based technologies. With my undying passion and curiosity, i have also worked on a graph database and some other projects in my spare time. I am also happy to tackle new challenges and i am always up to date with the trends in the industry.
